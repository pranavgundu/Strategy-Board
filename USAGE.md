# How to Use Strategy Board
## Accessing Strategy Board
First access Strategy Board from any of the following domains: 
- [`strategyboard.app`](https://strategyboard.app)
- [`strategyboard.vercel.app`](https://strategyboard.vercel.app)
- [`pranavgundu.github.io/Strategy-Board`](https://pranavgundu.github.io/Strategy-Board)
## Creating a New Match
1. Click on the green "New" button on the homepage. 

<p align="center">
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/22479a84-a6a8-496e-8faf-a74341d36379" alt="New Match Button" width="50%">
</p>

From there a popup will appear asking for match details. 

<p align="center">
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/22c4f170-bf2f-4e4e-82a4-d59c4c3d6a14" alt="Match Details Popup" width="50%">
</p>

2. Fill in the match details:
   - Match Name: Enter a name for your match. 
   - Team Numbers: Enter the team numbers for both alliances (Red and Blue). *The order that you put in the team numbers **will be the order they appear in the whiteboard**.*

<p align="center">
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8eaa5b4f-9655-4aa2-8974-e786ed253792" alt="Filling Out Match Details Popup" width="50%">
</p>

3. Click the "Create" button to proceed.
