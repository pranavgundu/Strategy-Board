## Accessing Strategy Board
First access Strategy Board from any of the following domains: 
- <a href="https://strategyboard.app" style="text-decoration: none; color: #3b82f6;">strategyboard.app</a>
- <a href="https://strategyboard.vercel.app" style="text-decoration: none; color: #3b82f6;">strategyboard.vercel.app</a>
- <a href="https://pranavgundu.github.io/Strategy-Board" style="text-decoration: none; color: #3b82f6;">pranavgundu.github.io/Strategy-Board</a>
## Creating a New Match
1. Click on the green "New" button on the homepage. 

<p align="center">
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/22479a84-a6a8-496e-8faf-a74341d36379" alt="Image" width="50%">
</p>

From there a popup will appear asking for match details. 

<p align="center">
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/22c4f170-bf2f-4e4e-82a4-d59c4c3d6a14" alt="Image" width="50%">
</p>

2. Fill in the match details:
   - Match Name: Enter a name for your match. 
   - Team Numbers: Enter the team numbers for both alliances (Red and Blue). *The order that you put in the team numbers **will be the order they appear in the whiteboard**.*

<p align="center">
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8eaa5b4f-9655-4aa2-8974-e786ed253792" alt="Image" width="50%">
</p>

3. Click the "Create" button to proceed.

<p align="center">
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e7f8ebe9-50c8-4591-b9a5-f4ea2622af41" alt="Image" width="50%">
</p>

This will create a match that you can access from the homepage.

<p align="center">
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9f7729b9-b56c-4b70-928c-a74eca8e72f7" alt="Image" width="50%">
</p>
