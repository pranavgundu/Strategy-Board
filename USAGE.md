You can access Strategy Board from any of the following domains: 
- <a href="https://strategyboard.app" style="text-decoration: none; color: #3b82f6;">strategyboard.app</a>

### Switching Sections on the Whiteboard

The whiteboard is divided into different sections for different parts of the match.
- Autonomous (**Auto**)
- Teleoperated (**Teleop**)
- **Endgame**
- **Notes**

Auto, Teleop, and Endgame, <u>have the same view as they are the same fields</u>, just for different parts of the match. *Notes* is a freeform section where you can jot down any additional information, as well as ranking points.

To switch between sections, click on the respective button on the top of the whiteboard.

<p align="center">
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/86ea6070-b95e-438e-82a3-f480592798ee" alt="Image" width="50%">
</p>

You can see what section you are by looking at what section is bolded (Auto).
